About Us

Our background

Cellware Scientific offers a series of revolutionary cell culture and microbial culture products. Our mission is to create innovative and user-friendly to help life science researchers obtain faster, better results. Our novel single-use bioreactor system is designed for adherent & non-adherent cell cultures. It utilizes disposable, flexible, cell culture bags with an active aeration device and is the only system that can fit into CO2 incubators. Our innovative design optimizes growth environments, improves cell yields, and increases throughput, all in an affordable and eco-friendly way. Our unique disposable culture tubes feature dual positioned flip-caps, allowing for quick and single handed operation.
